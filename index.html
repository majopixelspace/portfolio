03 The arc

From code, to design, and back again.

  1. Engineer

    I studied software engineering. I learned to build — logic, systems, code — before I ever learned to design.

  2. Designer

    I moved into UX/UI. For six years I've designed end‑to‑end products: research, IA, flows, high‑fidelity UI, and prototyping — across four countries.

  3. Both

    The two halves met. I design and build, prototyping directly in code with AI. What I hand off isn't a mockup — it's something you can use.

04 How I work

AI moves fast. The design judgment stays mine.

I collaborate closely with product and engineering teams — shaping flows and information architecture, running usability and accessibility reviews, turning findings into real changes. Then I prototype in code with AI. For a studio, that means faster validation and less back‑and‑forth between design and dev.
